Welcome to our Language School

01

Foreign language skills and learning opportunities have historically been of crucial importance to our students and are at the very heart of the GURU’s main purpose.


Our goal – is to teach you to speak a vivid foreign language used in everyday life.


GURU will help you to overcome the language barrier with qualified teachers who
organize inspiring learning process and motivate anyone to communicate.


The study of foreign languages with pleasure. Thanks to the easy maner of classes, you will practice perfectly spoken foreign language and learn the basic grammar easily. No boring lectures or methods! Only a positive outlook and the joy of learning.


A teacher is a guide on the path, a Guru is a pointer to the way
02

At first you will learn to use the gained knowledge in practice by developing all four language skills: reading, writing, conversation and listening.


To do this we create for you the language environment similar to a natural one: to speak, practice and learn!

03

We will make everything possible to ensure that lessons will be interesting and we will try to create a relaxed learning atmosphere.


This will provide the effective and natural assimilation of material.

Work


Before you enroll to a course, teachers will determine your proficiency in a foreign language. For this individual testing is conducted.

Classes can be held in groups (3 - 6 people), as well as individually or half-individually.

After passing the final test at the end of the level,you will be given a certificate indicating your level of knowledge on all aspects of language learning.

Training

Classes at GURU foreign language school are held twice or three times a week.
Duration of one lesson for adults and for teens - 1 hour 30 minutes, for children 1 hour (60 minutes).
Private lessons carry on according to your wishes!
